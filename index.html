PROJECT

The RFID-Based Attendance Monitoring with Payroll System of Mercedes Agripreneur Integrated Farm School Incorporated (MAIFSI) is designed to streamline attendance tracking and payroll processing through automation. This system utilizes Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology to accurately record employee attendance in real-time, reducing manual errors and ensuring a more efficient payroll computation. By integrating attendance data directly with the payroll system, it eliminates the need for tedious paperwork, minimizes fraudulent practices, and enhances productivity within the organization. This innovative solution not only improves accuracy and efficiency but also promotes punctuality and accountability among employees.

The RFID-Based Attendance Monitoring with Payroll System was successfully implemented at Mercedes Agripreneur Integrated Farm School Incorporated (MAIFSI), addressing the inefficiencies of manual attendance tracking and payroll processing. Through real-time RFID-based monitoring, the system significantly reduced paperwork, minimized errors, and improved payroll accuracy, leading to enhanced employee trust and satisfaction. The evaluation results demonstrated its effectiveness, with a highly positive weighted mean of 4.42 from respondents, confirming its reliability and efficiency. The implementation also showcased improved punctuality and productivity among employees, reinforcing the system's impact on streamlining operations. While minor challenges remain in optimizing payroll generation, this project highlights the importance of technology-driven solutions in enhancing workforce management within educational institutions. The accompanying images illustrate the real-world application of this system, providing a clear visual representation of its success and transformative impact at MAIFSI.
